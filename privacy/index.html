This Privacy Policy explains how PlaySat TV collects, uses and protects information when you use the PlaySat TV application on LG webOS TV.

Important: PlaySat TV is a media player only. The app does not provide, sell, host or distribute third-party TV channels, movies, series or IPTV subscriptions. Users are responsible for ensuring that any playlist or content source they use is legal and authorized.

1. About PlaySat TV

PlaySat TV is a media player application. The app allows users to add and use their own IPTV playlist credentials to access media content through a TV interface.

2. Information We Collect

a) Playlist Information

If you add a playlist, the app may store:

  • Playlist name
  • Server URL
  • Username
  • Password

This information is used to load your playlist inside the app.

b) Device and License Information

The app may use a device identifier generated from the TV/device environment or a platform-provided device identifier where available.

This identifier is used to:

  • Validate trial status
  • Validate subscription/license status
  • Restore license status after reinstalling the app on the same TV
  • Prevent repeated trial abuse

c) App Usage Settings

The app may store local settings such as:

  • Selected language
  • Favorites
  • Continue watching data
  • Hidden or selected groups
  • Parental/access settings

d) Technical Information

The app may communicate with PlaySat TV servers for:

  • License validation
  • Demo playlist functionality
  • App service functionality
  • Error or status responses needed for the app to work

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to operate and improve the app, including:

  • Loading playlists added by the user
  • Saving user preferences
  • Restoring app settings
  • Validating trial and license status
  • Providing demo content for testing and review
  • Improving app reliability and compatibility

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell user data.

Information may be processed by PlaySat TV servers only when needed for app functionality, license validation, demo mode or technical operation.

We do not provide third-party IPTV subscriptions and we do not intentionally share playlist credentials with unrelated third parties.

5. Playlist Credentials

Playlist credentials entered by the user are used only to access the playlist source provided by the user. Users should only enter playlist credentials from legal and authorized providers.

6. Demo Mode

The app includes a demo mode with legal sample content. Demo mode is provided for testing, review and demonstration purposes.

7. Data Storage

Some information may be stored locally on the TV, such as playlist details, favorites, settings and continue-watching data.

License-related information may be stored or checked on PlaySat TV servers to validate access and subscription status.

8. User Choices

Users may:

  • Remove or replace playlists inside the app
  • Clear app data through TV/app settings where available
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Contact support for privacy-related questions

9. Children’s Privacy

PlaySat TV is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Users are responsible for controlling access to content and playlists used in the app.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect app functionality and license validation. However, no internet-connected service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions may be published on our website or made available through the app or app store listing.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact:
